It also develops and manufactures branded molasses-based feed  supplements, in the form of high and low moisture feed blocks,  which enrich the diet of all types of ruminant farm animals. Operational Locations The division’s products are manufactured in the USA, Germany  and the UK and are sold through a vast distribution network  across the UK, Europe, New Zealand and North America. That network is expected to include South America in 2018  following the conclusion of farm trials.
